# Actions

**Actions** are items that are assigned to a Group Member and given a target date. All Members of the Group can see the Action and add Comments/Replies to help move tha Action along.

## Starting an Action

An **Action** can be started by any Group Member.

<center>
	<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/global/addAction.gif')} />
</center>

## Reporting Progress

The Group Member **asigned** the Action, or any other Group Member can add Comments/Replies at any time that the Action is in progress.

## Comments & Replies

### Adding Comments

**Comments** should be relative to the **Action** _(**Replies** should be specific to a **Comment**)_.

<center>
	<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/global/addingComments-2.gif')} />
</center>

### Add Files

Simply click the **Add File** tab and follow the instructions.

<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/global/addFiles-2.gif')} />

The **Files** are accessible from both the Files tab at the top of the Action as well as the Paper Clip icon next to the Comment.

### Adding Replies

When Replying to a Comment, you will see the Reply To This Comment tab. When you have added your Reply, you can also [add Files](#add-files) before submitting your Reply.

<center>
	<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/global/addingReplies-2.gif')} />
</center>

## Completing an Action

The **assigned** Group Member, or the Group Admin can click the **Click to Complete Action** button when the Action is completed.
