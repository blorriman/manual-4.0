# Notice of Meeting

A **Meeting** can be scheduled by a Group Administrator. He/She creates the agenda and then submits _(sends the Notice of Meeting)_ to the Group Members by email.

<center>
	<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/meetings/meetingNotice.jpg')} />
</center>

## Meeting Home Page

The Meeting Home Page is where everything associated with the Meeting happens.

<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/meetings/meetingHomePage.jpg')} />

## Adding to the Agenda

If the **Meetings** hasn't started, any Member can add Agenda Items to the Draft Agenda.

<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/meetings/addAgendaItem.gif')} />

Once the **Meeting** starts, you can add [Comments](/docs/started/General%20Usage/intro#adding-comments), [Replies](/docs/started/General%20Usage/intro#adding-replies) and [Vote](../voting/intro)
