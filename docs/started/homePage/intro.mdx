# Your Home Page

This is **Your Dashboard** for everything happening on **LORROS**

## Profile Management

A **LORROS** user profile is comprised of your username _(this is not social media so it is recommended that real names be used)_, email address _(all **LORROS Messages and Notices** will be sent to this address)_ and password _(minimum 6 characters)_.

:::info Info
All of this information can be updated from **Your Homepage**.
:::

### Change Name

It is always best pactice to use your real name, but you can change your name in **LORROS** at any time.

<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/started/change name.gif')} />

### Change Email

When you receive an invite to participate in a **LORROS Group**, you must create your own account. At that time, you can choose your preferred email address for all future **LORROS** communications.
You can change your **LORROS** email address at any time by editing your profile information _(see User Profile)_ . Once your profile is established, you are the only one with access to your profile.

<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/started/change email address.gif')} />

### Short Bio

If you wish, you can provide a brief resume to introduce yourself.

<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/started/short bio.gif')} />

### Change Password

**LORROS** easily allows you to change your password at any time.

<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/started/change password.gif')} />

### Change Avatar

It's always fun to add a small photo _(or avatar)_ of yourself which will be displayed beside all your comments.

### Language

**LORROS** is currently availabe in English and French. Your language will be automatically detected when you register, but you can change it any time.

<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/started/change language.gif')} />

### Unregister from Lorros

:::warning Warning
Unregistering from **LORROS** will delete your profile and remove you from all groups permanently!
:::

You may delete your **LORROS** profile at any time. From your home page, click _“Unregister”_ button. This will remove you from the **LORROS** database and all **LORROS Groups** to which you belong.

If you are the only one with Admin Access in any **LORROS Group**, a list will appear in the form. Before your registration can be cancelled, you must give another member of each of those Groups Admin Access.

If you are the last member of a **LORROS Group**, the Group will be removed from **LORROS** and all the group’s records will be deleted from the **LORROS** archives.

<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/started/unregister.gif')} />
