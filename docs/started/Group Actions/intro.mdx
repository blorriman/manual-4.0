# Group Home Page

This is the home page for each **Group** you belong to, there are a number of things you can do from here.

<!-- **LORROS** allows you to belong to as many groups as you want, they can all be accessed from your [Home Page](/docs/started/homePage/intro). -->

## Group Members

Access the **Group** members from the **Members** tab.

<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/group actions/groupMembers.gif')} />

### Send an Email

If you don't want to start a **Group Discussion**, and only want to send a _'one off'_ email to members of your Group, you can do it from the Group Home Page.

:::caution Caution
These emails are **NOT** saved in **LORROS**
:::

Email the whole **Group**

:::info info
You can also remove members individually by checking the **X** after their name.
:::

<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/group actions/emailAll.gif')} />

To email individual members, click the email box beside their name.

<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/group actions/emailOne.gif')} />
