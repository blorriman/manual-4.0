# Common Actions

**LORROS** is easy to use once you learn the basics. The following actions are common across all **LORROS** Activities.

## Adding Comments

Across **LORROS**, anytime you are allowed to add **Comments**, you will see the Add Comments tab. When you have added your Comments, you can also [add Files](#add-files-to-submissions) before submitting your Comment.

<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/general usage/addingComments-2.gif')} />

## Adding Replies

When Replying to a Comment, you will see the Reply To This Comment tab. When you have added your Reply, you can also [add Files](#add-files-to-submissions) before ubmitting your Reply.

<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/general usage/addingReplies-2.gif')} />

## Expanding Comments & Replies

To see all Comments and Replies, click the Expand All icon at the top right of the Comments. To collapse all Comments and Replies, the icon changes to Collapse All.

<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/general usage/expanding.gif')} />

## Sorting Comments

Comments and Replies are displayed in reverse chronological order _(last first)_, but if you want to view them in chronological order, click the _'order icon'_ at the top right of Comments. You can also toggle back to reverse chronological order.

<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/general usage/sorting.gif')} />

:::caution Tip
You may need to Expand All before Sorting to get the right sorting sequence.
:::

## Add Files to Submissions

Most submissions _(Comments, Replies, etc.)_ allow you to add files. Simply click the **Add File** tab and follow the instructions.

<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/general usage/addFiles.gif')} />

## Add Links to Submissions

In all **LORROS** submissions _(Comments, Replies, etc.)_, you can easily add **links**.

1. From the page you want to **link to**, copy the address.
1. In the **Submission**, select the text you want associated with the **link**.
1. Click the **link icon** in the submission toolbar.
1. Paste the copied link in the Enter link box.
1. Click **Save**

<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/general usage/addLink.gif')} />

## Opening Files

All **Files** for each Activity _(Discussion, Meeting, etc.)_ can be accessed from the Files tab at the top. The **Files** for each Comment _(Agenda, etc.)_ can be accessed from the Files icon _(paperclip)_.
