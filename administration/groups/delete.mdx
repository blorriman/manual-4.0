# Deleting a Group

To create a Group:

> 1. Open the **Admin** tab.
> 1. Open **Delete Group**.
> 1. Click **Delete Group**.

<center>
	<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/administration/del-1.gif')} />
</center>

:::warning ATTENTION!
Did we mention that this will **DELETE** the Group and all the Group data ...
:::
